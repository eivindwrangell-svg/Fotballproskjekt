    #1

    Ousmane Dembélé

    Ousmane Dembélé won the Ballon d’Or because he had an incredible season and proved himself as one of the best football players in the world. His speed, dribbling, and creativity made him very difficult for defenders to stop. He scored important goals and created many chances for his teammates in crucial matches. Dembélé also showed great consistency and performed well in both league and international competitions. Another reason he deserved the award was his hard work and determination. Over the years, he improved a lot as a player and became more mature on the field. His performances helped his team achieve major success throughout the season.

    #2

    Harry Kane

    Harry Kane finished second in the Ballon d’Or rankings after an outstanding season. He scored many important goals and proved himself as one of the best strikers in the world. Kane showed incredible consistency and helped his team in crucial matches throughout the year. His finishing, passing, and leadership made him a complete forward, not just a goal scorer. He was calm under pressure and always performed at a high level against strong opponents. Although he did not win the Ballon d’Or, Kane’s performances earned him great respect from fans and players around the world. He truly deserved his place among football’s elite.

    #3

    Lamine Yamal

    Lamine Yamal finished third in the Ballon d’Or rankings after an unbelievable season at such a young age. Even though he is still a teenager, he played with confidence, creativity, and maturity far beyond his years. His dribbling, speed, and technical skills made him one of the most exciting players in world football. Yamal consistently impressed in big matches and showed that he could compete with the very best players in the world. Finishing third in the Ballon d’Or at such a young age is an incredible achievement and proves that he has the potential to become one of football’s future legends.
